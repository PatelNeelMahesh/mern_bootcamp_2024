
## **3 Exercises to Convert into 1NF and 2NF**

---

### **Exercise 1 — Online Course Enrollment**

#### **Unnormalized Table**

| StudentID | StudentName | Courses               | Fees (₹)         | InstructorName   | InstructorPhone                    |
| --------- | ----------- | --------------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| S001      | Priya Shah  | HTML, CSS, JavaScript | 5000, 6000, 7000 | Neel, Neel, Neel | 9991112222, 9991112222, 9991112222 |
| S002      | Arjun Mehta | Python, C             | 7000, 8000       | Mahir, Mahir     | 9993334444, 9993334444             |

---

#### **Task**

1. Convert into **1NF** — ensure each cell holds a single value.
2. Identify a **unique key** for each record.
3. Convert into **2NF** by separating:

   * Student details
   * Course details
   * Enrollment relationships

### **Exercise 2 — Hospital Patient Record**

#### **Unnormalized Table**

| PatientID | PatientName   | DoctorName | DoctorSpeciality | Medicines             | Dosages    |
| --------- | ------------- | ---------- | ---------------- | --------------------- | ---------- |
| P001      | Krishna Patel | Dr. Yash   | Cardiologist     | Aspirin, Atorvastatin | 75mg, 10mg |
| P002      | Meera Joshi   | Dr. Rohan  | Neurologist      | Gabapentin            | 300mg      |
| P003      | Rajiv Rana    | Dr. Yash   | Cardiologist     | Aspirin, Metoprolol   | 75mg, 50mg |

---

#### **Task**

1. Convert into **1NF** — one medicine per row.
2. Identify **partial dependencies** and remove them to reach **2NF**.

### **Exercise 3 — Car Service Center**

#### **Scenario**

A car service center keeps track of each customer and the services done on their car.
Currently, all details are stored in a single table.

#### **Unnormalized Table**

| CustomerID | CustomerName  | CarModel    | ServiceNames                 | ServiceCost (₹) | MechanicName | MechanicPhone          |
| ---------- | ------------- | ----------- | ---------------------------- | --------------- | ------------ | ---------------------- |
| C001       | Rohan Patel   | Honda City  | Oil Change, Tyre Rotation    | 800, 500        | Amit, Amit   | 8881112222, 8881112222 |
| C002       | Yashvi Desai  | Tata Nexon  | Battery Check, Brake Service | 300, 1200       | Neel, Neel   | 8883334444, 8883334444 |
| C003       | Priyesha Shah | Hyundai i20 | Oil Change                   | 800             | Amit         | 8881112222             |

---

#### **Task**

1. Convert the table into **1NF** — each row should represent one service performed.
2. Identify a **composite key** (for example, `CustomerID + ServiceName`).
3. Remove **partial dependencies** to achieve **2NF**, by splitting the table into:

   * `Customers`
   * `Services`
   * `ServiceRecords`
